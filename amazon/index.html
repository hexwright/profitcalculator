Frequently Asked Questions — Amazon Seller Fees 2026

Amazon charges a referral fee of 8–15% on most categories, FBA fulfillment fees of $3.22–$14.50+ per unit, and a selling plan fee ($0.99/item Individual or $39.99/month Professional). On a $30 product with FBA, total Amazon fees typically range from $9–$12 depending on size and category.
Amazon referral fees in 2026 range from 6% to 45% depending on the product category. Most general merchandise categories are 15%. Electronics and Computers are 8%, Personal Computers 6%, Clothing 12%, Jewelry 20%, and Amazon Device Accessories up to 45%.
Amazon introduced a 3.5% fuel and inflation surcharge on FBA fulfillment fees starting April 17, 2026. This adds approximately $0.11–$0.51 per unit depending on your size tier. Our Amazon fee calculator includes this surcharge by default with a checkbox to turn it off if needed.
FBA (Fulfilled by Amazon) — Amazon stores, packs and ships your orders. You pay fulfillment fees ($3.22–$14.50+) but get the Prime badge which typically increases conversions 25–40%. FBM (Fulfilled by Merchant) — you handle all shipping yourself. Only the referral fee applies, but you cover shipping costs out of pocket.
If you sell more than 40 items per month, the Professional plan at $39.99/month works out cheaper than Individual at $0.99 per item. The Professional plan also unlocks Amazon Sponsored Ads, bulk listing tools, promotions, and access to the Buy Box — all critical for serious Amazon sellers.
Most successful Amazon sellers target 20–35% net profit margin after all fees and costs. Margins below 15% are considered risky — they leave no buffer for advertising spend, returns, refunds, or unexpected storage fees. Always run numbers through an Amazon fee calculator before sourcing any product.
Referral fee percentages are similar globally but FBA fulfillment fees vary by marketplace. UK and EU FBA fees are generally slightly higher than US rates. Use the currency selector in our calculator for your local currency and always verify the latest exact rates inside your Amazon Seller Central account.

Tips to Improve Your Amazon Profit Margin

📦

Optimize your FBA size tier

FBA fees jump between size tiers. Reducing packaging by even 1–2cm can move you to a lower tier and save $1–$2 per unit — which adds up to thousands per year at scale.

🏷️

Double-check your category listing

Wrong category means wrong referral fee. Electronics at 8% vs general merchandise at 15% is a 7% difference on every sale. Always verify your category is correct before going live.

📊

Switch to Professional plan early

At just 40 sales/month, Professional ($39.99) beats Individual ($0.99/item). It also unlocks Sponsored Ads and the Buy Box — both essential for growing your Amazon business.

⚡

Keep inventory at 30–60 days supply

Stock below 28 days triggers the Low Inventory Fee ($0.32–$1.11/unit). Stock over 90 days risks aged inventory charges. The sweet spot is 30–60 days of forecasted sales.
