Our Story

Why ProfitCalcu Exists

Every online seller has had that moment — you sell an item, feel good about the price, and then the platform fees, payment processing charges, and shipping costs quietly eat away at what you thought was a decent margin. By the time you count everything, your "profit" is almost nothing.

"Sellers shouldn't need a spreadsheet degree to figure out if they're actually making money. The math should be instant, accurate, and free."

That frustration is exactly what led to the creation of ProfitCalcu — a free, no-signup profit calculator built specifically for marketplace sellers. We cover the six biggest platforms where independent sellers do business: Etsy, Amazon FBA, eBay, Shopify, Alibaba, and AliExpress.

Our goal has always been simple: give sellers a fast, accurate answer to the one question that matters most — what is my real net profit after every fee?

Our Journey

How ProfitCalcu Has Grown

ProfitCalcu started as a single calculator for one platform and has grown into a full suite trusted by sellers across the globe.

The Beginning

One Problem, One Tool

The first ProfitCalcu calculator launched to solve a specific pain point: Etsy sellers not realizing how much the Offsite Ads fee and transaction fee combined were cutting into their margins.

Expanding

Amazon FBA & eBay Added

Seller requests made it clear that the same problem existed on Amazon and eBay. We built dedicated calculators for both — with full fee breakdowns unique to each platform.

Growing

Shopify, Alibaba & AliExpress

Dropshippers and wholesale importers joined the community. We expanded to cover Shopify store margins, Alibaba bulk import costs, and AliExpress dropship calculations.

2026

50,000+ Sellers and Growing

Today, ProfitCalcu is used by over 50,000 sellers worldwide. All six calculators have been fully updated for 2026 fee structures. More platforms — including Walmart Marketplace, TikTok Shop, and Mercari — are coming soon.
